Inputs & Regular Expressions

Username must be and contain 5 - 12 characters (letters and numbers only, case insensitive

Email must be a valid address. The local part can contain numbers, letters, periods, hyphens, and underscores, and be as long as necessary. Then there must be an '@'. The domain must be

Password must be at least 8 characters, and contain at least one upper case letter, one lower case letter, one number, and one special character (@$!%*?&_-)

Telephone must be a valid US telephone number (11 digits)
